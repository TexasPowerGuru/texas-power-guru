About Texas Power Guru

Texas Power Guru was founded to simplify the complicated Texas electricity market. Our proprietary and advanced technology allows Texans to search through the hundreds of electricity plans available and determine if you are on the most affordable energy plan available to you based on YOUR projected usage.

Our energy plans are simple, fixed rate plans with no bill credits or gimmicky price structures. All energy plans are sourced from the Public Utility Commission of Texas website, powertochoose.org.

And the best part? We're proud to offer a completely customizable analysis of the energy plans available in your area for an industry best $1.
