What We Do

Why we built Texas Power Guru, how it works, and what makes it different from every other electricity comparison tool.

The Problem We're Solving

Texas has one of the most competitive electricity markets in the world — but also one of the most confusing. Hundreds of plans from dozens of providers, each structured differently. Bill credits that make cheap-looking plans expensive at your actual usage. Tiered rates. Time-of-use pricing. Gimmicks designed to obscure the true cost.

The state's official comparison tool, PowerToChoose.org, shows rates at a generic 1,000 kWh benchmark — which may not reflect your home's usage at all. The result is that most Texans either overpay for electricity or spend hours trying to decode their options.

We built Texas Power Guru to fix that.

How It's Different

📐

Usage-Based Ranking

Plans are ranked by your projected annual cost — not a generic 1,000 kWh benchmark. Enter the months you know; we estimate the rest from Texas seasonal data.

🚫

No Gimmick Plans

We automatically filter out bill-credit plans, variable-rate plans, time-of-use plans, and prepaid plans. Only straightforward fixed-rate options make the list.

💰

No Commissions

We earn nothing from the providers we display. There is no financial incentive to show you one plan over another. Our only revenue is the optional $1 detailed report.

🔄

Updated Daily

Plan data is pulled directly from PowerToChoose.org every day. You're always seeing current rates, not cached data from last week.

California Creek Holdings, LLC holds a Retail Electric Provider Broker license issued by the Public Utility Commission of Texas. This license authorizes us to provide electricity brokerage services to Texas consumers and businesses — and is one of the key differentiators that allows us to offer analysis unavailable from unlicensed comparison tools.
